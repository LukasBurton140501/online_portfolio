Necopinatus

Necopinatus is a Unity based game that was created in my second year of University along side a group of five other students from different courses. Together we created this product and won a competition between the other students.

Zool

In my last year of University I worked with a group of fellow students to port the game Zool from Windows DX11 to Vulkan, so that it could be used on the Nintendo Switch when a Switch Development kit becomes available.

This project was licenced to the group I was a part of, by the company Sumo Digtal who wanted both an Orbis port and a Vulkan port of their licence Zool. I enjoyed working within a team immensely.
